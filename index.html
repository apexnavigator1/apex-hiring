Job overview

The role

As an Appointment Setter, you will be the first point of contact for our potential clients, responsible for scheduling appointments with prospective customers interested in our Amazon FBA training.

You'll work closely with our sales and marketing teams to qualify leads, nurture interest, and help optimize our funnel by driving webinar attendance and appointment bookings. This is a remote role ideal for someone with excellent English communication skills, a proactive mindset, and a willingness to learn, improve and apply high-end sales skills.
